# Concept A: "The Odometer"

## Hero Layout

Dark navy full-bleed. Left 55%: small kicker "Честный банк" above a massive **11%** in Display XL (Unbounded 900) that counts up from 0 via odometer animation — this IS the headline. Below it: "на накопительный счёт" in Golos Text + single blue CTA "Открыть счёт". Right 45%: the Agent Card mockup at a slight tilt, bleeding 15% past the container edge, with a subtle headlight-pulse glow on the card chip. Background: `road-foggy.png` at 20% opacity under navy gradient + noise.

## Scroll Narrative

1. **DARK** — Hero (11% odometer + card)
2. **WHITE** — Agent Card section (card features, 1.5% cashback as the number headline, asymmetric 2-card layout: featured card 1.5x wider)
3. **DARK** — Tariffs (3 cards in convoy animation, staggered entry, featured middle card elevated and larger)
4. **ACCENT** — Income Calculator (single Display XL number: "1 000 ₽ за каждого клиента", slider interaction, result animates green via conversion moment pattern)
5. **DARK** — AI Assistant (night-highway.png atmosphere, dashboard glow aesthetic, chat UI mockup)
6. **WHITE** — Deposits (rate comparison, numbers as headlines)
7. **DARK** — Trust Block (river-sunset.png, "9,2 млрд ₽" counting up, A+(RU) badge fade-in, 250+ offices)
8. **Footer**

## Screenshot Moment

The 11% odometer counting from 0 to 11 on page load — digits rolling like a car's mileage counter. No other Russian bank does this. The number IS the entire hero.

## What Makes It Different

The hero is stripped to its absolute essence — one number, one card, one CTA. Maximum confidence. The odometer mechanic gives automotive DNA without showing a car.
