# Brand — Astro-Volga Bank

## Name
**Astro-Volga Bank** (legal entity: JSC "Royal Credit Bank", Central Bank license #783)
Tagline: **"Honest Bank"**

## Group Connection
Part of "Dialect Group" alongside insurance company "Astro-Volga Insurance" (founded 1990, Samara):
- Premiums grew from 0.5B to 9.2B RUB (2016–2023)
- #1 in Central Bank customer loyalty ranking
- ACRA rating: A+(RU), outlook "Stable"
- 250+ physical offices across Russian regions
- Established agent network nationwide

Strategy: insurance agents become bank lead generators. Every insurance office gets a bank representative from existing agents.

## Positioning
**"Bank for those on the road"** — car owners and entrepreneurs from Russian regions.

Key meanings: honesty (transparent tariffs), automotive theme (gas station cashback, AstroDrive subscription), agent model (passive income), regional focus (deliberately not targeting Moscow/SPb).

## USP (with concrete numbers — always use these, never vague equivalents)
1. **Agent Card** — 1.5% cashback with no limits, 11% on savings account
2. **Agent Income** — 1,000 RUB per referred client + 10% of bank's monthly revenue from that client
3. **AI Assistant** — automated audience search, lead generation, targeted ads
4. **AstroDrive** — 300 RUB/month, roadside assistance, enhanced cashback
5. **Insurance + Banking** — unified ecosystem (car insurance + bank card + cashback on policy)

## Tone of Voice
- Simple language, no banking jargon
- Confident but not aggressive
- Numbers and specifics instead of promises — "11% on savings" not "great rates"
- No "premium" or "elite" positioning — we are about practicality
- Friendly to 30-55 age group from regions
- The NUMBER is the headline. Not a tagline. Not a slogan. The specific number.

## Visual Identity
- **GAZ-21 "Volga"** — emotional anchor, AI-generated illustrations and line-art. Heritage, not nostalgia.
- **Palette:** blue (#0067B1) dominates + navy (#1B1F3B) for authority + green (#27AE60) earned at conversion
- **Fonts:** Unbounded (display — character and confidence) + Golos Text (body — clean Russian design)
- **Atmosphere:** PNG assets layered under content, never flat backgrounds. Every section has depth.
- **Style:** clean, uncluttered, with a sense of quality. "Premium accessibility" — Revolut confidence meets regional trust.

## Competitive Differentiation (design context)
- We are NOT Sber (visual noise, 5 CTAs per screen, overwhelming)
- We are NOT Alfa (animation everywhere = animation nowhere)
- We are NOT T-Bank (storytelling for 25yo Moscow — our agent is 45, from Samara, wants numbers)
- We are NOT a generic bank template ("Reliable partner since 2005" — meaningless)
- We ARE: bold numbers + product mockups + choreographed scroll + automotive soul
